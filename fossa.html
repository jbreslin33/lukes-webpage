fossa

A fossa has claws like like cats
a long tail and round ears
although it shares some adaption
with cats, the fossa is closely related
to the mongooses and civets.
It's coat is short,usually a rich brown
with a golden tinge, the fossa's tail makes
up about a half of a mammal's length !
That extra long tail helps the fossa balance
and jump from branch to branch.
It has retractable claws like those of a
a cat help it climb down a tree head first.
